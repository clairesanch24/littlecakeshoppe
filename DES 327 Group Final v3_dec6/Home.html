There are times when all you need is a little dessert to stop your cravings or if you need more than just a bite and need to share and celebrate a special occation, why not order a cake!
I specialties on custom orders for birthdays, babyshowers, weddings, anniversaries, you name it! Little Cake Shoppe sweets are baked and decorated from home here in Oakland, CA. In the hopes that one day not only do online orders but as well a store front.
My passion for baking started with an Easy Bake Oven. As a kid, ive always loved helping my parents in the kitchen. Either by weighing, cleaning or stirring the pot. But i wanted to feel like i could do more then just the basics, i wanted to feel like i could cook or bake something on my own. And when i saw this easy bake oven in stores, the rest was history for me.
After school, i would love to bake for my friends, school events and family. My parents always supported me on baking even wishing that maybe one day ill have my own shop. Ones i graduated high school, knowing that my hobby could turn into a career i signed up to a culinary school here in San Francisco.
